    • About Troop 104

    Troop 104 is a Boy Scout troop based out of Port Huron, Michigan. We are a part of the Water and Woods district of the Michigan Crossroads Council. Troop 104 has spawned many Eagle Scouts in its history, including 8 in the past 18 months.


    We are led by Scoutmaster Joseph Thornton, and many assistant scoutmasters. Flag retirements, flag ceremonies, and clean-up projects are some the service projects the troop regularly partake in.
